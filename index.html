Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how "Mileage Your Journey" (the "App"), developed by VannepuRam (the "Developer"), handles your information. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App is designed to work offline. All the data you create, including your saved vehicles and journey history, is stored exclusively on your own device's local storage. We do not collect, see, or store any of this personal data on our servers. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time from within the app.

2. Data Collected by Third-Party Services

To provide core functionalities like advertisements and in-app purchases, we use third-party services that may collect information from you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services, but we are transparent about their use in our App.

a) Google AdMob

The free version of our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google. To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and use data, including but not limited to:

  • Device Information: Such as your device model and operating system.
  • Device or other IDs: Including your device's unique Advertising ID.
  • Approximate Location: Inferred from your IP address to show geographically relevant ads.
  • App Interactions: Data on how you interact with the ads shown in the App.

For more information on how Google collects and uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

b) Google Play Billing

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. This transaction is processed securely through the Google Play Billing service. To manage this purchase, Google collects necessary data, including:

  • Personal Info: Your Google Account email address to associate the purchase with your account.
  • Financial Info: Your purchase history to verify your ad-free status and allow for purchase restoration.

We do not have access to your payment information (such as credit card numbers), as this is handled entirely by Google's secure payment system.

c) Google Translate

The App uses the Google Translate widget to provide multi-language support. When you use this feature, the text content of the App may be sent to Google's servers for translation purposes. This may involve the collection of your IP address and other diagnostic data by Google.

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your data at any time. Since all your primary data (vehicles, history) is stored locally on your device, you can delete it using the following methods:

  • In-App Deletion: Use the "Delete" button for individual vehicles or the "Clear History" button within the app to remove your data.
  • System Deletion: You can uninstall the app from your device, which will delete all of its locally stored data. Alternatively, you can clear the app's data through your phone's system settings (Settings > Apps > Mileage Your Journey > Storage > Clear data).

4. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: vannepuapps@gmail.com
